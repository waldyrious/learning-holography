About this site

These pages host a collection of notes, diagrams and animations that I've been developing as aids to my study of holography and specifically computer-generated holography (CGH). I'm making them publicly available, hoping they might help others who are curious about the topic.

Requirements for previous knowledge of mathematics, physics or signal processing will be kept as low as possible. My goal is to maintain a highly accessible resource for the curious layman.

I make no claim of scientific accuracy or otherwise correctness of these pages! I'm still learning myself :)

The sources for this site are maintained on Github. Feel free to make suggestions or corrections, either via issue reports or pull requests. Feedback can otherwise be sent to <waldyrious@gmail.com> and I'll gladly respond.

Thanks for stopping by!
